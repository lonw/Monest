ABOUT MONEST

Centum Financial Group Inc. is a network of independently owned and operated mortgage broker firms located across Canada. Federally incorporated in 2002, the CENTUM network is home to over 260 franchised mortgage centres with more than 2500 mortgage professionals.
The mission of Centum Financial Group Inc. is to continue to be the leading Mortgage Brokerage Operation in Canada, setting the standard in the industry for professionalism, dependability and reliability for its customers.
We provide our clients variety of options on the best way to process foreign exchange transactions depending on the specific client's needs. Our specialists in the foreign money exchange markets will consider every particular case whether buying an overseas property or making regular and one-off international payments.
As a member of Centum Financial Group, Monest Financial has been No.1 group in Centum Financial across Canada since 2009.
The 2010 top award winners for CENTUM Financial Group Inc. were recognized at the Annual Awards and Recognition events, taking place on April 6, 2011. Monest Financial was awarded #1 Franchisee in Canada by mortgage dollar volume. There are 35 brokers won the National Gold Award, 6 are from Monest financial; 80 brokers won the National Silver Award, 8 are from Monest Financial; there are 4 brokers from Monest Financial among the Top 10 National and Ontario agents.
"It is always the best rate with the best services”, the President of Monest Financial, Susan Wang, said the ranking of company performance is not the most important. Sincere cooperation between her team and the company continues steady development is she mostly cares. The biggest expectation of Monest Financial is to keep the unity and cooperation among brokers in order to win customers' satisfaction and the banks' trust.
