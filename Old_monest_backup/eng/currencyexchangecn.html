Requirements

Monest Currency Exchange offers the following methods of payment: Cash Business Cheque (regular/certified) Bank Draft
Monest Currency Exchange operates in accordance with the requirements of Canadian Federal Law, which stipulates that all cash transactions equal to or exceeding CAD $3,000.00 have to be accompanied with a proper proof of identity document. In addition, all cash transactions equal to or exceeding CAD $10,000.00, must include a declaration completed by the customer.
Acceptable identification documents include Driver's License, Passport, Birth Certificate, Permanent Resident Card, Citizenship Card, or similar government issued identification displaying the following: Full legal name Date of birth Photo All identification documents must be current and original (No photocopies are accepted). Neither Ontario Health Card nor credit cards are considered proper identification.
