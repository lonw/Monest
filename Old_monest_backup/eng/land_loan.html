Land Loan

When the borrowers are planning to purchase a piece of land and the amount of their capital is not enough to cover the price, they would like to borrow from financial institutions or individual investors. The borrower could construct buildings or resell the land when the price of the land goes up. The collateral of land loan is the land going to be purchased or other property have the identical value to protect the investors' interest.
